Feature overview

Turn student experience into a guided support network.

Ambassador Management lets a university invite, manage, and showcase ambassadors. Students discover mentors in the mobile app, while external ambassador flows support web-based prospect engagement where configured.

What the client gets

When Active, the module unlocks ambassador dashboard cards, sidebar lists, invite flows, ambassador detail profiles, mobile ambassador carousel, and external ambassador discovery where enabled. When inactive, ambassador tools and discovery surfaces are hidden.

FAQs

Ambassador Management questions clients usually ask.

Is this only for enrolled students?
No. Internal ambassadors support enrolled or incoming students in the app, while external ambassador surfaces can support prospect engagement where enabled.
Does Chat need to be active?
Ambassador discovery can exist as a module, but direct messaging uses the Chat module when the university wants in-product conversations.
What happens when inactive?
Ambassador menus, invite paths, profiles, carousel discovery, and external ambassador access are hidden.
