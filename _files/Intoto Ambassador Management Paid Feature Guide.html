Feature overview

Turn student experience into a guided support network.

Ambassador Management lets a university invite, manage, and showcase ambassadors. Students discover mentors in the mobile app, while external ambassador flows support web-based prospect engagement where configured.

What the client gets

When Active, the module unlocks ambassador dashboard cards, sidebar lists, invite flows, ambassador detail profiles, mobile ambassador carousel, and external ambassador discovery where enabled. When inactive, ambassador tools and discovery surfaces are hidden.

FAQs

Ambassador Management questions clients usually ask.

What is Ambassador Management?
Ambassador Management is the paid module for inviting, managing, and showcasing student mentors and external ambassadors. It helps students and prospects connect with people who can answer real campus and onboarding questions.
Is this only for enrolled students?
No. Internal ambassadors support enrolled or incoming students in the app, while external ambassador surfaces can support prospect engagement where enabled.
What is an internal ambassador?
An internal ambassador is usually a current or enrolled student who represents the university experience inside Intoto. They help incoming students understand campus life, academic expectations, culture, and practical next steps.
What is an external ambassador?
An external ambassador supports prospect-facing or off-platform recruitment conversations where the university has that model enabled. This is useful before a student is fully onboarded into the main app experience.
How does a student find an ambassador?
Students can browse ambassador cards, open profile details, compare interests or program context, and choose someone relevant. The mobile discovery carousel makes this visible without requiring staff to manually introduce every student.
What information appears on an ambassador profile?
A profile can include photo, name, program, campus, interests, languages, bio, experience, and contact entry points where Chat is Active. The goal is to help students choose the right person before starting a conversation.
Does Chat need to be Active?
Ambassador discovery can exist as a module, but direct in-product messaging uses the Chat module. If the university wants students to message ambassadors inside Intoto, Chat should also be Active.
Can students ask ambassadors questions?
Yes, where Chat and the correct permissions are Active. Students can ask practical questions about arrival, academics, housing, student life, clubs, culture, and onboarding.
Who invites ambassadors?
University Super Admins or authorized ambassador administrators invite ambassadors according to the university's operating model. This keeps representation controlled by the institution.
Can the university remove an ambassador?
Yes. Ambassador visibility and access are governed by admin controls. If someone should no longer represent the university, staff can remove or suspend access according to policy.
Can ambassadors help with admissions or onboarding?
Yes. Ambassadors are useful for admissions reassurance, peer guidance, arrival preparation, campus culture, student life, and informal questions that staff may not answer as naturally.
How does Ambassador Management help sales or recruitment?
It gives prospects and incoming students a human connection to the university. Instead of only reading brochures, they can learn from real student voices and trusted representatives.
Can Ambassador Management work with Community?
Yes. Ambassadors can participate in relevant communities where the university permits it. Community gives group discussion, while Ambassador Management gives discoverable mentor profiles.
Can Ambassador Management work without External Ambassadors?
Yes. A university can use only internal student ambassadors if that is the right program model. External ambassador functionality can be treated as a separate enabled surface where configured.
What happens when inactive?
Ambassador menus, invite paths, profiles, carousel discovery, student mentor discovery, and external ambassador access are hidden for that university.
