Feature overview

Arrival operations without spreadsheet chasing.

Travel Plan gives universities a clean view of student arrival details, travel timelines, and campus arrival status. Staff can see who has submitted details, who is in transit, and who needs follow-up.

What the client gets

When Active, the module unlocks Travel Plan dashboard cards, sidebar navigation, campus travel lists, status filters, student travel records, and arrival monitoring. When inactive, travel screens and campus travel tabs are hidden.

FAQs

Travel Plan questions clients usually ask.

What information does the student provide?
Typical travel details include arrival date/time, campus, airport or station, flight/train number, transport mode, accommodation or check-in notes, and emergency contact information.
Can staff see students who have not submitted plans?
Yes. The value of the module is visibility into both submitted plans and missing plans, so teams can follow up early.
What happens when inactive?
Travel Plan cards, sidebar entries, travel lists, campus travel tabs, and arrival-status workflows are hidden.
